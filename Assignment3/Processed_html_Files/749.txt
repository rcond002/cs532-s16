and SkyDrive Pro is now OneDrive for Business. Read more about this change at From SkyDrive to OneDrive.\nMicrosoft Publisher 2013 offers new ways to work with your pictures, so you can move them around, swap them, and add visual pop to your publications with new picture and text effects.\n\n\nWorking with pictures\nPublisher 2013 lets you search for pictures in one place, whether they\xe2\x80\x99re on your computer, in the Office.com clip art gallery, or on the web.\n\n\n\n\nAdding pictures\nPublisher 2013 makes it easier to work with batches of photos. When you choose several pictures at one time, Publisher places them in a column in the   scratch area. \nFrom the scratch area you can drag a picture onto the publication page and drag it back off again, or swap one picture for another if you don\xe2\x80\x99t like the way a layout looks.\n\n\n\n\n\nSwapping pictures\nTo swap two pictures, either both on the page or with one in the scratch area, select one picture and drag it by the mountain icon to the other picture until a pink highlight appears around the picture. Release the mouse button and the swap is complete.\n\n\n\n\n\nCreating picture backgrounds\nYour pictures make impressive backgrounds for your publication pages. From within your publication, right-click a picture, choose Apply to Background, and then click either Fill to expand the picture to take up the entire background, or Tile to fill your background with tiles of your picture.\n\n\n\n\n\n\nPicture effects\nChoose from the many new picture effects available in Publisher 2013 to apply shadows, reflections, glow, soft edges, bevels, and 3-D rotations to your pictures. \nSelect the picture and click Picture Effects on the Picture Tools \xe2\x80\x93 Format tab.\n\n\n\n\n\nText effects\nYou can also apply new effects to the text in your publications: Choose from new shadows, reflections glows, and bevels. Select the text and click Text Effects on the Text Box Tools \xe2\x80\x93 Format tab.\n\n\n\n\n\nPhoto center printing\nOnline photo printing options have never been so convenient, and now you can save your publications specifically for photo printing. Each page of your publication is saved as a JPEG picture that you can upload to a photo center website for printing.\n\n\n\n\n\nMore information\n\n\n\nBasic tasks in Publisher 2013\n\n\n\n\nCrop the picture to a shape\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nApplies To: Publisher 2013\n\n\n\n\n\r\n\t\r\n\xef\xbb\xbf\r\n\n\n\n\n\nWas this information helpful?\n\nYes\nNo\n\n\n\nHow can we improve it?\n255 characters remaining\n\n\nTo protect your privacy, please do not include contact information in your feedback.
