regularly get  new and improved features. If you\'re an Office 365 Home, Personal, or University subscriber, learn more about how to upgrade to Office 2016. If you\'re an Office 365 for Business subscriber, learn more about how to get the new Office 2016 apps.\nInterested in  being one of the first people to try out new features and functionality? If you\'re an Office 365 Home, Personal, or    University subscriber, you can choose to become an Office Insider. \n\n\nJanuary 2016 Featured Updates\nTake a look at the table below to find out about some of the features that are being added this month as well as get links to additional content to help you take advantage of these improvements and additions. For a visual tour of what\'s new this month, check out the video below.\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nInk Annotations\n\n\nIn Word, Excel, and PowerPoint\n\nUse your finger or pen to write and draw, and use the tools on the new Draw tab to highlight content of particular interest. \nLearn more about drawing and annotating with ink.\n\n\n\nBlack theme\n\n\nIn Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook, and OneNote\n\nUse this new theme to get the highest level of contrast while working in your Office apps. \nLearn more about  changing the look and feel of Office 2016 apps with themes.\n\n\n\nNew Charts\n\n\nIn Excel\n\nFunnel charts can be used to help you display values as progressively decreasing proportions. \nLearn more about creating a funnel chart.\n\n\n\n\n\nNew functions\n\n\nIn Excel\n\nAvoid nested IF functions with the new IFS, MAXIFS, MINIFS, and SWITCH functions.\n\n\n\nData entry  made easier\n\n\nIn Excel\n\nSpeed up formula entry with the CONCAT and TEXTJOIN functions.\n\n\n\nMore Send As Options\n\n\nIn Excel\n\nSend a document either as an attachment or as a PDF without leaving the confines of  your worksheet from the Share pane.\nLearn more about sharing your workbook with others.\n\n\n\n\n\nImproved Formula  Autocomplete\n\n\nIn Excel\n\nOnly remember a part of a formula name (for example, you know if contains a \xe2\x80\x9crand\xe2\x80\x9d or a \xe2\x80\x9cvalue\xe2\x80\x9d somewhere)? No problem! Excel now returns all partial matches for everything you type\xe2\x80\x94not just exact matches from the beginning of the string.\n\n\n\nReal-Time Presence\n\n\nIn PowerPoint\n\nWhen collaborating with others on a presentation, see not only who is working on the presentation but also what slide each person is working on.  \nLearn more about collaborating on PowerPoint presentations with real time co-authoring.\n\n\n\nFinding contacts made easier\n\n\nIn Outlook\n\nEver looked for a contact and realize that couple of them have the same name? You will now be able to pick the right one as Outlook will show you not only the names but also the picture of your contacts.\nLearn more about finding people and contacts.\n\n\n\n\n\nClick an icon below to learn more about what\'s new in a particular app.\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nNOTE: If you use Office 2016 at work, the availability of the features above may vary based on your company\'s update policies.\n\n\nNovember 2015 Featured Updates\nTake a look at the table below to find out about some of the features that are being added this month as well as get links to additional content to help you take advantage of these improvements and additions. As you use it, Designer  learns about your preferences and adapts its recommendations to be tailored to you.\nLearn more about the PowerPoint Designer\n\n\n\nCalendar Insights Template\n\n\nIn Excel\n\nView your calendar as a dashboard and drill into the data get a better handle on how you spend your time and identify ways to get more out of your days.\nLearn more about the Calendar Insights template\n\n\n\n\n\nFinancial Templates\n\n\nIn Excel\n\nTake advantage of couple new templates to track what  you earn, how much you spend, and where your spending occurs as well as quickly analyze and compare the performance of selected stocks over time.  \nLearn more about the My Cashflow template, and the Stock Analysis template\n\n\n\nMore Send As Options\n\n\nIn Word and PowerPoint\n\nSend a document either as an attachment or as a PDF without leaving the confines of  your document from the Share pane.\nLearn more about sharing documents in  Word and presentations in PowerPoint\n\n\n\nInsert Online Video\n\n\nIn OneNote\n\nLiven up your notes by adding online videos and bringing interactivity to your content.\nLearn more about inserting online videos\n\n\n\n\n\nClick an icon below to learn more about what\'s new in a particular app.\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nNOTE: If you use Office 2016 at work, the availability of the features above may vary based on your company\'s update policies.\n\n\n\n\n\n\nSeptember 2015\n\n\n\n\n\nCo-Authoring\n\n\nIn Word and PowerPoint\n\nWork with others simultaneously on a document regardless of the device you\'re using.\nLearn more about co-authoring in Word and PowerPoint\n\n\n\nSimplified Sharing\n\n\nIn Word, PowerPoint, and Excel\n\nJust click the Share button in the Ribbon for easy sharing right from your Office documents. See who has access to a given document and who is currently working in the document, and change individual authoring permissions for any document you own.\nLearn more about sharing in Word,  PowerPoint, and Excel\n\n\n\nShared Notebooks\n\n\nIn OneNote\n\nCollect any and all relevant information (e.g., photos, videos, clippings, drawings,...) in one central location, work with others, and watch the app sync changes within seconds.  \nLearn more about sharing a OneNote notebook with other people\n\n\n\n\n\nReal-Time Typing\n\n\nIn Word\n\nAs you collaborate with others in a document, see where others are working and view their edits as they happen.\n\n\n\nModern Attachments\n\n\nIn Outlook\n\nAttach a document  from your recent items and share them from OneDrive or SharePoint with email recipients. Also configure sharing permissions so that all the recipients have access to the attached file without having to leave the app.\nLearn more about attaching a file to an email\n\n\n\nMail Triage\n\n\nIn Outlook\n\nThe Clutter feature learns  how you prioritize your mail and then helps you by putting low priority messages in a separate folder (while still giving you a daily summary so you don\'t miss anything). When on the go, you can also take advantage of this capability by using the Focused Inbox.  \nLearn more about using Clutter to sort low priority messages\n\n\n\n\n\nImproved Version History\n\n\nIn Word, PowerPoint, and Excel\n\nRefer back to previous snapshots and earlier drafts  of documents during the editing process as you collaborate with others.\nLearn more about viewing historical versions of Office files\n\n\n\nOne-Click Forecasting\n\n\nIn Excel\n\nWith one simple click, create forecast charts based on historical data and predict future trends. This new capability uses the industry standard Exponential Smoothing (ETS) algorithm to give you reliable forecasting data.  \nLearn more about creating a forecast\n\n\n\nNew Chart Types\n\n\nIn Word, PowerPoint, and Excel\n\nVisualize financial or hierarchical  data, and highlight statistical properties of your data with new chart types: Treemap, Waterfall, Pareto, Histogram, Box and Whisker, and Sunburst. \n\n\n\n\n\nExport Data Source Information to Excel\n\n\nIn Access\n\nGet a list of all the linked data sources from your Access database application into Excel for reference.\nLearn more about exporting linked data source information to Excel\n\n\n\nMore Flexible Timelines\n\n\nIn Project\n\nNot only leverage multiple timelines to illustrate different phases or categories of work, but also set the start and end dates for each timeline separately, to paint a clearer overall picture of the work involved.\nLearn more about creating multiple timelines.\n\n\n\nBetter Control over Resource Scheduling\n\n\nIn Project\n\n Negotiate an agreement, called a resource engagement, to make sure that resources with limited availability are being used appropriately and effectively throughout your organization.\nLearn more about resource engagements.\n\n\n\n\n\nImproved Data Connectivity\n\n\nIn Visio Professional\n\nConnecting your diagram to Excel data is now just one step away. Make sure there is a one-to-one match between values in an Excel column and the text for each shape on the diagram, and simply click Quick Import on the Data tab.\n\n\n\nModern Shapes\n\n\nIn Visio\n\nTake advantage of the many redesigned shapes that are now available. In Visio Professional, the Basic Electrical template now has shapes that are compliant with the IEEE standard. In Visio Standard, the Office Layout shapes have been updated. Also check out the Starter diagrams to quickly get going.\n\n\n\nBetter Together with Windows 10\n\n\nIn Word, PowerPoint, Excel, Mail, Calendar, and OneNote\n\nTake the Office mobile apps for a spin and find out why they are the perfect option for on-the-go productivity. They help you do great work anywhere, anytime, with documents in full fidelity across all your Windows 10 devices.    \r\n \r\n\n\n\n\n\n\nOffice Themes\n\n\nIn Word, PowerPoint, Excel, OneNote, Outlook, Access, Project, Visio, and Publisher\n\nPick the  Office theme that\'s right for you. The Dark Gray theme provides a high contrast look and feel that is easy on the eyes. The Colorful theme offers a modern and fresh look. The White theme provides a traditional Office look.\nLearn more about changing the Office theme\n\n\n\nTell Me\n\n\nIn Word, PowerPoint, Excel, Outlook, Project, Visio, and Access\n\nSimply type what you want to do in the app using your own words, and then Tell Me will guide through the process as well as offer additional resources.  \nLearn more about doing things quickly with Tell Me\n\n\n\nSmart Lookup\n\n\nIn Word, PowerPoint, Excel, and Outlook\n\nFact-check or explore terms in your documents with Bing-powered Smart Lookup. Simply highlight terms in your document and use this feature to bring in search results from the web right into your reading or authoring environment.\nLearn more about getting insights into what you\'re working on\n\n\n\n\n\nOneDrive Integration\n\n\nIn Word, PowerPoint, Excel, OneNote, and Outlook\n\nAccess your Office documents from anywhere and any device by saving them to  OneDrive, pick up anywhere you left off, and co-author with others from the comfort of your office or home, or even on the go.\nLearn more about saving documents online.\n\n\n\nSkype Integration\n\nUse Skype to check in and collaborate with someone using  Instant Messaging (IM), voice, video, or screen sharing. Also join online meetings while on the go using the Skype for Business mobile app. See who is online, IM meeting participants, and follow along when a presenter shares his screen - all from your tablet or phone.\n\n\n\nCross-Platform, Cross-Device\n\n\nIn Word, PowerPoint, Excel, OneNote, and Outlook\n\nEasily switch from one device to the next without missing a beat.   View and edit your Office documents across Windows, Android, and Apple devices. Review, edit, analyze, and present with a consistent look and familiar user experience across your devices.\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nApplies To: Excel 2016, Word 2016, Outlook 2016, PowerPoint 2016, OneNote 2016, Publisher 2016, Access 2016, Visio Professional 2016, Visio Standard 2016, Project Professional 2016, Office 2016, Project Standard 2016\n\n\n\n\n\r\n\t\r\n\xef\xbb\xbf\r\n\n\n\n\n\nWas this information helpful?\n\nYes\nNo\n\n\n\nHow can we improve it?\n255 characters remaining\n\n\nTo protect your privacy, please do not include contact information in your feedback.
