Read more about this change at <a class="ocpExternalLink" href="http://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/p/?linkid=390737" target="_blank" title="From SkyDrive to OneDrive">From SkyDrive to OneDrive</a>.</p>\n<p>Microsoft Publisher 2013 offers new ways to work with your pictures, so you can move them around, swap them, and add visual pop to your publications with new picture and text effects.</p>\n</section>\n<section class="ocpSection">\n<h2>Working with pictures</h2>\n<p>Publisher 2013 lets you search for pictures in one place, whether they\xe2\x80\x99re on your computer, in the Office.com clip art gallery, or on the web.</p>\n<p>\n<img alt="Insert Pictures" src="https://osiprodeusodcspstoa01.blob.core.windows.net/en-us/media/6f1bdbe2-f7cd-485b-8e9e-49531f685340.png" title="Insert Pictures"/>\n</p>\n<section class="ocpSection">\n<h3>Adding pictures</h3>\n<p>Publisher 2013 makes it easier to work with batches of photos. When you choose several pictures at one time, Publisher places them in a column in the   scratch area. </p>\n<p>From the scratch area you can drag a picture onto the publication page and drag it back off again, or swap one picture for another if you don\xe2\x80\x99t like the way a layout looks.</p>\n<p>\n<img alt="Scratch area" src="https://osiprodeusodcspstoa01.blob.core.windows.net/en-us/media/1c17b79e-6658-4624-85c2-57a2b5d23c4b.jpg" title="Scratch area"/>\n</p>\n</section>\n<section class="ocpSection">\n<h3>Swapping pictures</h3>\n<p>To swap two pictures, either both on the page or with one in the scratch area, select one picture and drag it by the mountain icon to the other picture until a pink highlight appears around the picture. Release the mouse button and the swap is complete.</p>\n<p>\n<img alt="Swap pictures" src="https://osiprodeusodcspstoa01.blob.core.windows.net/en-us/media/6a1bd174-da54-45d9-a89e-ca8f1c912c1a.jpg" title="Swap pictures"/>\n</p>\n</section>\n<section class="ocpSection">\n<h3>Creating picture backgrounds</h3>\n<p>Your pictures make impressive backgrounds for your publication pages. From within your publication, right-click a picture, choose <b class="ocpUI">Apply to Background</b>, and then click either <b class="ocpUI">Fill</b> to expand the picture to take up the entire background, or <b class="ocpUI">Tile</b> to fill your background with tiles of your picture.</p>\n<p>\n<img alt="Apply to background" src="https://osiprodeusodcspstoa01.blob.core.windows.net/en-us/media/a37b2db4-7674-47ad-8a6f-eee48e1ecc59.jpg" title="Apply to background"/>\n</p>\n</section>\n</section>\n<section class="ocpSection">\n<h2>Picture effects</h2>\n<p>Choose from the many new picture effects available in Publisher 2013 to apply shadows, reflections, glow, soft edges, bevels, and 3-D rotations to your pictures. </p>\n<p>Select the picture and click <b class="ocpUI">Picture Effects</b> on the <b class="ocpUI">Picture Tools</b> \xe2\x80\x93 <b class="ocpUI">Format</b> tab.</p>\n<p>\n<img alt="Picture effect" src="https://osiprodeusodcspstoa01.blob.core.windows.net/en-us/media/e1352691-4888-41e5-b85c-ced37433f8d4.jpg" title="Picture effect"/>\n</p>\n</section>\n<section class="ocpSection">\n<h2>Text effects</h2>\n<p>You can also apply new effects to the text in your publications: Choose from new shadows, reflections glows, and bevels. Select the text and click <b class="ocpUI">Text Effects</b> on the <b class="ocpUI">Text Box Tools</b> \xe2\x80\x93 <b class="ocpUI">Format</b> tab.</p>\n<p>\n<img alt="Text effects" src="https://osiprodeusodcspstoa01.blob.core.windows.net/en-us/media/1b1a920a-21bf-4b47-a110-1d358f9f5a30.jpg" title="Text effects"/>\n</p>\n</section>\n<section class="ocpSection">\n<h2>Photo center printing</h2>\n<p>Online photo printing options have never been so convenient, and now you can save your publications specifically for photo printing. Each page of your publication is saved as a JPEG picture that you can upload to a photo center website for printing.</p>\n<p>\n<img alt="Save for Photo Printing"
