class="ocpArticleContent" id="ocpArticleContent">\n<!--Last Published: 2/12/2016 8:11:28 AM PST-->\n<h1>What\'s new in Word 2016</h1>\n<section class="ocpIntroduction">\n<p>Word 2016 for Windows has all the functionality and features you\'re used to, plus  some enhancements and the best new features from Office 2016. </p>\n<p>Here are some of the top new features.</p>\n</section>\n<section class="ocpSection">\n<h2>Do things quickly with Tell Me</h2>\n<p>\r\n            You\'ll notice a text box on the ribbon in Word 2016 that says <b class="ocpUI">Tell me what you want to do</b>. This is a text field where you can enter words and phrases about what you want to do next and quickly get to features you want to use or actions you want to perform. You can also use Tell Me to find help about what you\'re looking for, or to use Smart Lookup to research or define the term you entered. </p>\n<p>\n<img alt=\'Shows the "tell me" search box on the ribbon in Word.\' src="https://osiprodeusodcspstoa01.blob.core.windows.net/en-us/media/fbd918d8-32f3-404c-ab65-9ae77170c29e.png" title=\'Shows the "tell me" search box on the ribbon in Word.\'/>\n</p>\n</section>\n<section class="ocpSection">\n<h2>Work together in real time</h2>\n<p>If you store your document online at OneDrive or SharePoint, and then share it with colleagues who use Word 2016 or Word Online, you can see each others\' changes to the document as they happen. After you save the document online, click <b class="ocpUI">Share</b> to generate a link or an email invitation. When your teammates open the document and agree to automatically share changes, you\'ll see their work in real time.</p>\n<p>\n<img alt="Can edit and Share options are highlighted in the Share dialog box" src="https://osiprodeusodcspstoa01.blob.core.windows.net/en-us/media/855a6593-071d-4f74-befb-5fddb635c334.png" title="Can edit and Share options are highlighted in the Share dialog box"/>\n</p>\n<p>\n<img alt="Other People Are Editing This Document prompt is shown from Share command" src="https://osiprodeusodcspstoa01.blob.core.windows.net/en-us/media/444e4d41-c17e-4109-adc6-d715ebf6c299.png" title="Other People Are Editing This Document prompt is shown from Share command"/>\n</p>\n<p>To learn how to collaborate in Word 2016, see <a class="ocpArticleLink" href="Collaborate-on-Word-documents-with-realtime-coauthoring-7dd3040c-3f30-4fdd-bab0-8586492a1f1d" title="Collaborate on Word documents with real-time co-authoring">Collaborate on Word documents with real-time co-authoring</a>.</p>\n</section>\n<section class="ocpSection">\n<h2>Insights into what you\'re working on</h2>\n<p>Smart Lookup, powered by Bing, brings research directly into your Word 2016. When you select a word or phrase, right-click it, and choose <b class="ocpUI">Smart Lookup</b>, the Insights pane opens with definitions, Wiki articles, and top related searches from the web.  </p>\n<p>\n<img alt="Smart Lookup is highlighted when you right-click some text or an image" src="https://osiprodeusodcspstoa01.blob.core.windows.net/en-us/media/3b97cfea-17e5-423d-adfc-26c0e88df321.png" title="Smart Lookup is highlighted when you right-click some text or an image"/>\n<img alt="Shows the Insights pane in Word" src="https://osiprodeusodcspstoa01.blob.core.windows.net/en-us/media/d8c77a4f-0834-4a6b-b5be-3fc551a8c2d2.png" title="Shows the Insights pane in Word"/>\n</p>\n<p></p>\n</section>\n<section class="ocpSection">\n<h2>Ink equations</h2>\n<p>Including math equations has gotten much easier. Now you can go to <b class="ocpUI">Insert</b> &gt; <b class="ocpUI">Equation</b> &gt; <b class="ocpUI">Ink Equation</b> any time you want to include a complex math equation in your document. If you have a touch device, you can use your finger or a touch stylus to write math equations by hand, and Word 2016 will convert it to text. (If you don\'t have a touch device, you can use a mouse to write, too). You can also erase, select, and correct what you\'ve written as you go.</p>\n<p>\n<img alt="The Equation option is highlighted on the Insert tab" src="https://osiprodeusodcspstoa01.blob.core.windows.net/en-us/media/921809de-f05a-46f0-a7b5-555af25a51ff.png" title="The Equation option is highlighted on the Insert tab"/>\n</p>\n<p>\n<img alt="Shows the ink equation pane in Word" src="https://osiprodeusodcspstoa01.blob.core.windows.net/en-us/media/fee5df2c-b895-4a40-a7e0-166ccf6255d2.png" title="Shows the ink equation pane in Word"/>\n</p>\n</section>\n<section class="ocpSection">\n<h2>Improved version history</h2>\n<p>Now you can go to <b class="ocpUI">File</b> &gt; <b class="ocpUI">History</b> to see a complete list of changes that have been made to your document and to access earlier versions. </p>\n</section>\n<section class="ocpSection">\n<h2>Simpler sharing</h2>\n<p>Click <b class="ocpUI">Share</b> to share your document with others on SharePoint, OneDrive, or OneDrive for Business, or to send a PDF or copy as an email attachment, directly from Word.</p>\n<p>\n<img alt="The Share icon is highlighted on the right side of the ribbon" src="https://osiprodeusodcspstoa01.blob.core.windows.net/en-us/media/fb65e293-c1db-4740-8fc6-2e4299ccb947.png" title="The Share icon is highlighted on the right side of the
