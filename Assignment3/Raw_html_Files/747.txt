class="ocpArticleContent" id="ocpArticleContent">\n<!--Last Published: 2/12/2016 7:35:25 AM PST-->\n<h1>What\'s new in OneNote 2016 for Windows</h1>\n<a name="__top"> </a>\n<section class="ocpIntroduction">\n<p>If you\'re already using <a class="ocpExternalLink" href="http://www.onenote.com/" target="_blank" title="OneNote">OneNote</a>, you know it\'s a digital notebook great for capturing, storing, and sharing all kinds of information. Depending on whether you\'re <a class="ocpArticleLink" href="upgrading-from-2010-or-2013-d22943e9-4c4e-4b4c-bbeb-2aa533e71ea1" title="upgrading from 2010 or 2013">upgrading from 2010 or 2013</a>, this version of OneNote will seem familiar, or totally new. Here\'s a list of what\'s new:</p>\n</section>\n<section class="ocpSection">\n<h2>Upgrading from 2013</h2>\n<p>If you\'re upgrading to OneNote 2016 from OneNote 2013, you\'ll still have all the features you\'re used to\xe2\x80\x94and you\'ll notice a few new ones as well. The first thing you\xe2\x80\x99ll see when you open OneNote 2016 is that it has the familiar look of 2013 with a more colorful border. You\'ll be able to take advantage of OneNote integration with other apps and services too.</p>\n<p>\n<b class="ocpNote">\n<include item="note"></include> Note </b>\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0If you\'re working on a Windows 10 computer and notice two versions of OneNote, you can read about   <a class="ocpExternalLink" href="A624E692-B78B-4C09-B07F-46181958118F" target="_blank" title="the difference between OneNote and OneNote 2016">the difference between OneNote and OneNote 2016</a>.</p>\n<section class="ocpSection">\n<h3>Insert videos into a page</h3>\n<p>You can now embed videos from Office Mix, Vimeo and YouTube into a page in OneNote. For more about how to do this, see <a class="ocpExternalLink" href="0a862f29-665c-43a5-9dd8-68009423cf7c" target="_blank" title="Insert online videos into OneNote">Insert online videos into OneNote</a>.</p>\n</section>\n<section class="ocpSection">\n<h3>Take notes right on the web</h3>\n<p>With Microsoft Edge, the new browser for Windows 10, you can write on the web. After you take notes, or highlight on a webpage, you can share your handiwork to OneNote. Learn more <a class="ocpExternalLink" href="http://windows.microsoft.com/en-us/windows-10/getstarted-write-on-the-web" target="_blank" title="at the Windows How-to site">at the Windows How-to site</a>.</p>\n</section>\n<section class="ocpSection">\n<h3>Send notes to yourself in email</h3>\n<p>With <b class="ocpLiteral">me@onenote.com</b>, you can send content from your inbox right to OneNote.\r\n\r\nSending email to OneNote is a great way to store important content so you can find it quickly later. If you\xe2\x80\x99re on the go and want to send yourself a reminder, just write a  quick mail. Or, if you\xe2\x80\x99re using an app with an "Email this" button, you can share it with yourself in email.</p>\n</section>\n<section class="ocpSection">\n<h3>Clip the web with Clipper</h3>\n<p>\n<img alt="Screenshot of OneNote Clipper dialog box." src="https://osiprodeusodcspstoa01.blob.core.windows.net/en-us/media/4568f591-96f4-4ce9-85d6-fb02343e5709.png" title="Screenshot of OneNote Clipper dialog box."/>\n</p>\n<p>OneNote Clipper is another way to clip the web right to OneNote. When you open Clipper, you can choose to clip an entire page or just part of one. You can even choose what notebook and section to clip it to\xe2\x80\x94so you\'ll know where to find it later. Clipper is a free app that you can get from <a class="ocpExternalLink" href="http://www.onenote.com/clipper" target="_blank" title="onenote.com/clipper">onenote.com/clipper</a>. Clipper sits on your browser\'s Favorites bar so it\'s at your fingertips when you need it.</p>\n</section>\n<section class="ocpSection">\n<h3>Capture images on your phone with Office Lens</h3>\n<p>Office Lens is a phone app. It\'s a great way to capture notes and info from whiteboards, menus, signs, or anything with a lot of text. You don\'t need to jot down notes, rely on blurry pictures, or worry about misplacing your notes. It\xe2\x80\x99s great for capturing sketches, drawings, and equations too, and even images without text.\r\n\r\nOffice Lens gets rid of shadows and odd angles so images are easier to read. You can download Office Lens for free from the <a class="ocpExternalLink" href="http://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/?linkid=392792" target="_blank" title="Windows Phone Store">Windows Phone Store</a>.\r\nDon\'t have a Windows Phone? No worries! Office Lens is available for <a class="ocpExternalLink" href="http://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/?linkid=526214" target="_blank" title="iPhone">iPhone</a>\r\nand <a class="ocpExternalLink" href="https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.microsoft.office.officelens" target="_blank" title="Android">Android</a> too.</p>\n</section>\n</section>\n<a name="bm1"> </a>\n<section class="ocpSection">\n<h2>Upgrading from 2010</h2>\n<p>If you\'re upgrading to OneNote 2016 from 2010, you\'ll enjoy all the features listed above, and you\'ll find a host of other new features that were introduced in 2013. OneNote has a clean and simple look so you can focus on your notes, and not the user interface. Aside from visuals, you\'ll have lots of useful features too.</p>\n<p>\n<b class="ocpNote">\n<include item="note"></include> Note </b>\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0If you\'re working on a Windows 10 computer and notice two versions of OneNote, you can read about   <a class="ocpExternalLink" href="A624E692-B78B-4C09-B07F-46181958118F" target="_blank" title="the difference between OneNote and OneNote 2016">the difference between OneNote and OneNote 2016</a>.</p>\n<section class="ocpSection">\n<h3>Save and share files in the cloud</h3>\n<p>\n<img alt="Save and share your notes in the cloud" src="https://osiprodeusodcspstoa01.blob.core.windows.net/en-us/media/f9d63ce2-30f3-473d-aad3-902b1aba72a8.png" title="Save and share your notes in the cloud"/>\n</p>\n<p>The cloud is your file storage in the sky. In OneNote, it\xe2\x80\x99s easy to <a class="ocpArticleLink" href="create-a-notebook-on-OneDrive-8de0408e-48cc-4216-9df2-b57eca486c77" title="create a notebook on OneDrive">create a notebook on OneDrive</a> or to <a class="ocpArticleLink" href="your-organizations-site-6dd65ee9-260f-428e-bb50-7c8f16972c27" title="your organization\xe2\x80\x99s site">your organization\xe2\x80\x99s site</a>. You can also use the free Office Online in virtually any browser.</p>\n</section>\n<section class="ocpSection">\n<h3>Draw, sketch, or handwrite notes</h3>\n<p>Smoothly <a class="ocpArticleLink" href="draw-09133d53-2e0b-4e2f-8b9c-9572a6d3b997" title="draw">draw</a>, erase, and edit with your finger, stylus, or mouse on any touch-capable device, such as a Tablet PC or laptop with touch screen. If you need to share your handwritten notes and care about legibility, OneNote can automatically <a class="ocpArticleLink" href="convert-your-handwriting-to-text-0ec88c54-05f3-4cac-b452-9ee62cebbd4c" title="convert your handwriting to text">convert your handwriting to text</a>.</p>\n<p>\n<a class="ocpTopOfPage" href="#top" title="Top of Page">Top of Page</a>\n</p>\n</section>\n<section class="ocpSection">\n<h3>Embed Excel spreadsheets and Visio diagrams</h3>\n<p>You can <a class="ocpArticleLink" href="attach-just-about-any-computer-file-8eba84c5-88c0-4505-8e5d-e82f5118b8a9" title="attach just about any computer file">attach just about any computer file</a> to any part of your notes, so you have a copy of the file in your notebook. You can also create or import <a class="ocpArticleLink" href="Excel-spreadsheets-a8ccd3fc-d61e-4913-b2a0-195d66599d92" title="Excel spreadsheets">Excel spreadsheets</a> and <a class="ocpArticleLink" href="Visio-diagrams-d36c2408-5e8c-44b6-b152-1b22b83d3462" title="Visio diagrams">Visio diagrams</a> right within OneNote and edit their information in place in your notes.</p>\n</section>\n<section class="ocpSection">\n<h3>Make better tables</h3>\n<p>Create more sophisticated tables in your notes than ever before. OneNote now supports new <a class="ocpArticleLink" href="formatting-options-22c66462-50c6-4ded-8bf0-ee1019047327" title="formatting options">formatting options</a> for cell shading, header rows, and data sorting within table cells so you can organize and display information the way you want to.</p>\n<p>\n<a class="ocpTopOfPage" href="#top" title="Top of Page">Top of Page</a>\n</p>\n</section>\n<section class="ocpSection">\n<h3>See who\xe2\x80\x99s collaborating</h3>\n<p>Identify and authenticate other authors more easily with the integrated identity profiles in OneNote. Use Instant Search to find anything you\xe2\x80\x99ve ever created or saved in OneNote.
