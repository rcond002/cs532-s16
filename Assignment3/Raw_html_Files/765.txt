foremost, your Windows Phone lets you make and receive phone\n\t\t  calls to stay in touch with people. Whatever you\'re doing, you can call people\n\t\t  in just a few taps\xe2\x80\x94and if you have\n\t\t  <a data-xmtag="hhtTopicLink" href="../cortana/meet-cortana">Cortana</a> on your\n\t\t  phone, she can help you\n\t\t  <a data-xmtag="hhtTopicLink" href="../cortana/what-can-i-say-to-cortana">call\n\t\t  someone</a>, too. To learn more about calling, including how to send\n\t\t  text replies, make international calls, forward calls, use speed dial, or call\n\t\t  someone from your call history, see\n\t\t  <a data-xmtag="hhtTopicLink" href="../calling-and-messaging/calling-options">Calling\n\t\t  options</a>. \n\t\t</p>\n<div class="note"><h3 data-xmtag="hhtSubheading" id="">Note</h3>\n<div data-xmtag="hhtMessage">\n<p data-xmtag="hhtPara">\n\t\t\t To make and receive phone calls, you need an account with a mobile\n\t\t\t phone operator and a working cellular signal in your current location. \n\t\t  </p>\n<p data-xmtag="hhtPara">\n</p></div>\n</div>\n<div autocollapse="" class="procedure" expandcollapsecontent="">\n<h3 data-xmtag="hhtSubheading" id="">To make a call using the keypad</h3>\n<ol data-xmtag="hhtSteps"><li data-xmtag="hhtStep">\n<p data-xmtag="hhtPara">On <span class="emphasis" data-xmtag="hhtUI">Start </span><img alt="Start icon" class="inlineImage" src="https://assets.windowsphone.com/6bb2095a-8baa-4fc5-b4ae-4745107b1070/hardware-icon-start-button_InvariantCulture_Default.png"/>, tap <span class="emphasis" data-xmtag="hhtUI">Phone </span><img alt="Phone Tile" class="inlineImage" src="https://assets.windowsphone.com/c0db9cc3-315a-41bc-9696-20125f91b92d/start-icon-phone_InvariantCulture_Default.png"/>.</p>\n</li><li data-xmtag="hhtStep">\n<p data-xmtag="hhtPara">Tap <span class="emphasis" data-xmtag="hhtUI">Keypad </span><img alt="Keypad icon" class="inlineImage" src="https://assets.windowsphone.com/cf4a7e63-0103-4e9e-be69-7d8c30610b6e/basic-icon-dialpad_InvariantCulture_Default.png"/>, enter a phone number, and then tap <span class="emphasis" data-xmtag="hhtUI">Call</span>.</p>\n</li></ol>\n</div>\n<div autocollapse="" class="procedure" expandcollapsecontent="">\n<h3 data-xmtag="hhtSubheading" id="">To call a contact</h3>\n<ol data-xmtag="hhtSteps"><li data-xmtag="hhtStep">\n<p data-xmtag="hhtPara">On <span class="emphasis" data-xmtag="hhtUI">Start </span><img alt="Start icon" class="inlineImage" src="https://assets.windowsphone.com/6bb2095a-8baa-4fc5-b4ae-4745107b1070/hardware-icon-start-button_InvariantCulture_Default.png"/>, tap <span class="emphasis" data-xmtag="hhtUI">People </span><img alt="People Tile" class="inlineImage" src="https://assets.windowsphone.com/ffd05690-5fe7-4b33-bdfc-03ae92958f12/start-icon-people_InvariantCulture_Default.png"/>, and then swipe over to <span class="emphasis" data-xmtag="hhtUI">Contacts</span>. (On Windows Phone 8, swipe over to <span class="emphasis" data-xmtag="hhtUI">All</span>.)</p>\n</li><li data-xmtag="hhtStep">\n<p data-xmtag="hhtPara">Tap the name of the person you want to call, and then tap the number to call.</p>\n</li></ol>\n</div>\n<div class="image" data-xmtag="hhtImage"><img alt="Call a contact on Windows Phone 8" src="https://assets.windowsphone.com/c6043a84-7346-4147-9006-e99c3ef8214f/phone-call-contact-400_InvariantCulture_Default.gif"/></div>\n<div class="tip"><h3 data-xmtag="hhtSubheading" id="">Tip</h3>\n<div data-xmtag="hhtMessage">\n<p data-xmtag="hhtPara">\n\t\t\t Have a friend you call a lot? You can\n\t\t\t <a data-xmtag="hhtTopicLink" href="../settings-and-personalization/pin-apps-to-start">pin\n\t\t\t a contact to Start</a>, then tap their Live Tile to make a quick\n\t\t\t call or send a text. Their Tile will show you new voicemails and more. \n\t\t  </p></div>\n</div> <h4 data-xmtag="hhtSubHeading2" id="">To answer a call</h4>\n<p data-xmtag="hhtPara">\n\t\t  If your phone is locked when you get an incoming call, slide up, and\n\t\t  then tap <span class="emphasis" data-xmtag="hhtUI">Answer</span>. If your phone is unlocked, tap\n\t\t  <span class="emphasis" data-xmtag="hhtUI">Answer</span>. To send an incoming call directly to voicemail, tap\n\t\t  <span class="emphasis" data-xmtag="hhtUI">Ignore</span>. \n\t\t</p>\n<div class="tip">\n<h3 data-xmtag="hhtSubheading" id="">Tip</h3> <div data-xmtag="hhtMessage"> <p data-xmtag="hhtPara">Can\'t talk\n\t\twhen you get a call? You can\n\t\t<a data-xmtag="hhtTopicLink" href="../calling-and-messaging/calling-options">text a\n\t\treply</a> instead.</p> </div> </div>\n<div autocollapse="" class="procedure" expandcollapsecontent="">\n<h3 data-xmtag="hhtSubheading" id="">To make an emergency phone call</h3> <ol data-xmtag="hhtSteps"><li data-xmtag="hhtStep"> <p data-xmtag="hhtPara">On <span class="emphasis" data-xmtag="hhtUI">Start </span><img alt="Start icon" class="inlineImage" src="https://assets.windowsphone.com/6bb2095a-8baa-4fc5-b4ae-4745107b1070/hardware-icon-start-button_InvariantCulture_Default.png"/>, tap\n\t\t<span class="emphasis" data-xmtag="hhtUI">Phone </span><img alt="Phone Tile" class="inlineImage" src="https://assets.windowsphone.com/c0db9cc3-315a-41bc-9696-20125f91b92d/start-icon-phone_InvariantCulture_Default.png"/>.</p> </li><li data-xmtag="hhtStep"> <p data-xmtag="hhtPara">Tap\n\t\t<span class="emphasis" data-xmtag="hhtUI">Keypad </span><img alt="Keypad icon" class="inlineImage" src="https://assets.windowsphone.com/cf4a7e63-0103-4e9e-be69-7d8c30610b6e/basic-icon-dialpad_InvariantCulture_Default.png"/>,\n\t\tenter the local emergency phone number, and then tap\n\t\t<span class="emphasis" data-xmtag="hhtUI">Call</span>.</p> </li></ol>\n</div>\n<div class="note">\n<h3 data-xmtag="hhtSubheading" id="">Note</h3> <div data-xmtag="hhtMessage"> <p data-xmtag="hhtPara"> If your\n\t\tphone has a password, you or someone else can still make an emergency phone\n\t\tcall without entering the password.
