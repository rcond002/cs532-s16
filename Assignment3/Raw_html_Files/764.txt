Store</a>, Microsoft\'s virtual entertainment shop for your phone.\n\t\t  Many apps are totally free, and others let you try before you buy. \n\t\t</p>\n<p data-xmtag="hhtPara">\n\t\t  You can shop the Store from your phone, and\n\t\t  <a data-xmtag="hhtForwardLink" href="http://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/?linkid=619684">browse the Store from your PC, Mac, or\n\t\t\t tablet</a>. \n\t\t</p>\n<div phone</div></div>\n<div class="note"><h3 data-xmtag="hhtSubheading" id="">Notes</h3>\n<div data-xmtag="hhtMessage">\n<ul data-xmtag="hhtList"><li data-xmtag="hhtListItem">\n\t\t\t\tThis topic is about Windows Phone 8.1. If your phone is running\n\t\t\t\tWindows Phone 8, some options and icons may look different, and some features\n\t\t\t\tmay not be available. Check to see\n\t\t\t\t<a data-xmtag="hhtTopicLink" href="../basics/which-version-of-windows-phone-do-i-have">which software\n\t\t\t\tversion you have</a> and\n\t\t\t\t<a data-xmtag="hhtTopicLink" href="../basics/how-do-i-update-my-phone-software">find out\n\t\t\t\tif an update is available</a>. \n\t\t\t </li><li data-xmtag="hhtListItem">\n\t\t\t\tTo\n\t\t\t\t<a data-xmtag="hhtTopicLink" href="../music-and-videos/get-music-videos-and-podcasts">get\n\t\t\t\tmusic, videos, and podcasts</a>, in the\n\t\t\t\t<a data-xmtag="hhtTopicLink" href="../settings-and-personalization/wheres-the-app-list">App\n\t\t\t\tlist</a> tap <span class="emphasis" data-xmtag="hhtUI">Music</span>, <span class="emphasis" data-xmtag="hhtUI">Video</span>, or\n\t\t\t\t<span class="emphasis" data-xmtag="hhtUI">Podcasts</span>. \n\t\t\t </li></ul>\n<p data-xmtag="hhtPara">\n</p></div>\n</div>\n</div><div data-xmtag="hhtBody">\n<div autocollapse="" class="procedure" expandcollapsecontent="">\n<h3 data-xmtag="hhtSubheading" id="">To shop from your phone</h3>\n<ol data-xmtag="hhtSteps"><li data-xmtag="hhtStep">\n<p data-xmtag="hhtPara">In the <a data-xmtag="hhtTopicLink" href="../settings-and-personalization/wheres-the-app-list">App list</a>, tap <span class="emphasis" data-xmtag="hhtUI">Store </span><img alt="Store Tile" class="inlineImage" src="https://assets.windowsphone.com/6babba13-994d-41cb-b8e6-9c6b71e9c105/start-icon-marketplace_InvariantCulture_Default.png"/>. If you know what you\'re looking for, you can also tap <span class="emphasis" data-xmtag="hhtUI">Search </span><img alt="Search icon" class="inlineImage" src="https://assets.windowsphone.com/5f2e61b6-8dfa-4638-8312-ecb0cea3b634/basic-icon-search_InvariantCulture_Default.png"/>.
