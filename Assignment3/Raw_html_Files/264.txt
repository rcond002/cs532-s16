[Sunday night] he broke the white board during the first quarter because we were playing like trash, pretty much.\xc2\xa0</p>\n</p>\n<p>\n<p>It doesn\xe2\x80\x99t matter what our record is or how great we\xe2\x80\x99ve been playing up until that point, we still have to keep a level of focus.
