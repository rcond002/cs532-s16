type="text/javascript">googletag.display(\'div-gpt-ad-1361440187065-4\')</script>\n</div>\n</div>\n<div class="articleContent">\n<p>National Signing Day has come and gone, although it\xe2\x80\x99s just the tip of the icebergs as colleges stock up on future talent for this fall.</p>\n<p>One big element to the recruiting race is players transferring, whether between DI schools or players making the move up from lower divisions.</p>\n<p>Here are seven transfers to keep an eye out as they setup shop at new homes.</p>\n<p><strong>GK <a href="/college-soccer/college-player-profile/arielle-schechtman/cpid-10033064">Arielle Schechtman</a>, <a href="/college-soccer/college-soccer-details/women/ucla/clgid-266">UCLA</a> to <a href="/college-soccer/college-soccer-details/women/georgetown/clgid-40">Georgetown</a></strong></p>\n<p>One of a handful of goalkeepers to see minutes last fall, Schechtman had some moments of brilliance in goal for the Bruins in what turned out to be a rather disappointing campaign for the team. The West Coast native is heading east for the nation\xe2\x80\x99s capital, where she\xe2\x80\x99ll join one of the Big East\xe2\x80\x99s most prominent programs, and stake a claim on the No. 1 shirt vacated by the graduated <a href="/college-soccer/college-player-profile/emma-newins/cpid-12496">Emma Newins</a>.</p>\n<p><strong>F <a href="/college-soccer/college-player-profile/julia-hernandez/cpid-93086">Julia Hernandez</a>, <a href="/college-soccer/college-soccer-details/women/eastern-florida-state-college/clgid-2415">Eastern Florida State College</a> to <a href="/college-soccer/college-soccer-details/women/ucla/clgid-266">UCLA</a></strong></p>\n<p>Overshadowed in UCLA\xe2\x80\x99s recruiting class by the premier U.S. and Canadian talent, Hernandez has the looks of a very solid depth option for the UCLA attack.
