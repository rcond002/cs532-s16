However, if you do not purchase a subscription or product, your access to NYTimes content and services will be limited.</p>\n<p>You can review the various NYTimes digital subscriptions <a href="http://www.nytimes.com/subscriptions/Multiproduct/lp87JHJ.html?campaignId=466K8">here</a>. We also offer other products and services, such as <a href="http://www.nytimes.com/pages/crosswords/index.html">crosswords</a>, <a href="http://www.nytimes.com/subscriptions/Multiproduct/lp4021.html?campaignId=393LW">gift subscriptions</a>, e-books and articles from our archive. Processing and Payment</strong></p>\n<p>The New York Times will process your purchase as promptly as possible. There may be a delay in the activation of your product while payment details are verified.</p>\n<p>We accept major credit cards and PayPal. We reserve the right to reject any order or purchase at any time.</p>\n<p>If your initial payment authorization is later revoked, your subscription, product or access will be terminated. <a href="https://myaccount.nytimes.com/membercenter/help-online-paid-products.html">Contact our Customer Care group</a> if you believe your access was terminated in error.</p>\n<p>Unless specified otherwise in the <a href="#cancel">Cancellation and Refund Policy</a>, all charges are nonrefundable.</p>\n<p>To view or change your payment method, <a href="https://myaccount.nytimes.com/membercenter/myaccount.html">visit the My Account area of NYTimes.com</a>.</p>\n<p id="pricing"><strong>1.4. Pricing</strong></p>\n<p>When you purchase a digital product, the price will be made clear during the order process. You agree to pay the price that is stated at the time of your order, as well as any applicable taxes. You also agree to the billing frequency stated at the time of your order.</p>\n<p>Discount eligibility is determined at the time of the order. Discounts cannot be applied retroactively.</p>\n<p>All prices are in United States Dollars, unless otherwise stated.</p>\n<p>The New York Times reserves the right to change prices and fees at any time. We will notify you in advance if the regular rate of a product changes from what was stated at the time of your order. You will have the opportunity to accept the new price or cancel your subscription or purchase from that point forward.</p>\n<p>Applicable taxes may vary. We are not able to notify you in advance of changes in applicable taxes.</p>\n<p>If a stated price is determined by us in our sole discretion to be in error, we are not under any obligation to offer you the product at that price. We will notify you of the error and give you the opportunity to cancel your order and obtain a refund if payment has already been made.</p>\n<p><em>Additional Costs</em></p>\n<p>Your subscription may not include access to all areas of the digital product, and you may have to pay additional fees to fully access those products. These additional charges will be clearly stated.</p>\n<p>When you use your digital product, you may incur other additional charges from third party service providers, such as telecommunications fees, data fees or service provider fees. You are responsible for paying any additional charges.</p>\n<p>If you believe someone else has used your account or you are being charged for a product you do not have, please <a href="https://myaccount.nytimes.com/membercenter/help-online-paid-products.html">contact our Customer Care group</a>.</p>\n<p id="billing"><strong>1.5. Billing of Digital Subscriptions</em></p>\n<p>We will charge or debit your payment method at the beginning of your subscription or, if applicable, at the end of your free trial period. Billing will continue according to the cycle stated at the time of your order.</p>\n<p>To view information about your billing cycle, <a href="https://myaccount.nytimes.com/membercenter/myaccount.html">visit the My Account area of NYTimes.com</a>.</p>\n<p>All NYTimes digital subscriptions are renewed automatically. When we renew your subscription, we will use the payment method currently associated with your account. If you redeem a gift subscription and choose to extend your subscription at the time of redemption, your subscription will renew automatically. You can view your payment method at any time in the <a href="https://myaccount.nytimes.com/membercenter/myaccount.html">My Account area of NYTimes.com</a>.</p>\n<p>You will not be notified in advance of impending renewals. Please see our <a href="#cancel">Cancellation and Refund policy</a> below for information about canceling.</p>\n<p id="nonsub"><em>1.5.2. Billing of One-Time Purchases</em></p>\n<p>When you make a one-time purchase of a standalone product, including but not limited to articles from the New York Times Archives and gift subscriptions, we will charge or debit your payment method at the time of purchase.</p>\n<p id="currency"><em>1.5.3. Currency</em></p>\n<p>The currency in which you will be billed will be stated during the purchase process and is determined based on your billing address.</p>\n<p id="unpaid"><strong>1.6. Promotions cannot be combined.</p>\n<p>You are required to provide your payment details when you sign up for a promotion. At the end of the promotion, you will automatically be signed up for the related subscription or purchase that will begin at the end of the promotional period. We will not notify you in advance that the promotion is about to end. To cancel and avoid being charged, you must notify us before the promotion ends.</p>\n<p id="software"><strong>1.8. You will not receive a refund for the current billing cycle. You will continue to have the same access and benefits of your product for the remainder of the current billing period.</p>\n<p>We reserve the right to issue refunds or credits at our sole discretion. Your access and privileges will continue to the end of the current billing period, and you will not receive a refund.</p>\n<p>We reserve the right to issue refunds or credits at our sole discretion. If we issue a refund or credit, we are under no obligation to issue the same or similar refund in the future.</p>\n<p><em>Changes to Content or Access</em></p>\n<p>We reserve the right to make changes to our digital products at any time. If we issue a refund or credit, we are under no obligation to issue the same or similar refund in the future.</p>\n<p id="changingsub"><strong>2.2.
